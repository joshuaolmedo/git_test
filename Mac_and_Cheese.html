Mac and Cheese!

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups of milk
  • 12 ounces of macaroni
  • 7 ounces of cheddar cheese

Steps:

  1. In a pot, put in 4 cups of milk
  2. Put in medium heat, we are going to make it slightly boil. Stir once in a while for the milk to not burn.
  3. Once in a boil, put 12 ounces of pasta.
  4. On medium low heat, let it cook for about 10-15 minutes until all milk is absorbed by the pasta.
  5. Now, we take it off and add 12 ounces of cheddar cheese.
  6. Lastly, we are going to incorporate everything until the cheese has melted.
