Pink Lemonade Fudge!

Ingredients:

  • three cups of melted chocolate
  • 14 ounces of condensed milk
  • two packets of pink lemonade Kool-Aid
  • sprinkles

Steps:

  1. In a bowl put in three cups of melted chocolate, 14 ounces of condensed milk, and two packets of pink lemonade Kool-Aid
  2. Give it a good mix and pour the fudge into baking dish, then add sprinkles.
  3. In another bowl, put on the potatoes and mix 1/2 corn starch, 1 cup of mozzarella cheese and a pinch of salt
  4. Give it a fat 1-2 hours to cool down until set
