Potato Cheese Sticks!

Ingredients:

  • 2 russet potatoes
  • 1/2 corn starch
  • mozzarella cheese
  • salt

Steps:

  1. Peel your potatoes and roughly chop them off
  2. Boil them up until super soft
  3. In another bowl, put on the potatoes and mix 1/2 corn starch, 1 cup of mozzarella cheese and a pinch of salt
  4. Afterwards, pop in the freezer for 30 minutes
  5. Fry until golden brown
